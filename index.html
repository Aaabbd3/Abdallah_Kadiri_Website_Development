Story

Your story begins at the precipce of chaos. A power hungry tyrant has taken control of the world, and his experiments run rampant across it, however the people have yet to truly see the problems that surround them. They still live in a false peace, and it is up to you to prevent this impending storm from sending the world into chaos.

Project Half-Soul is a 2d rougelike game where you play an android in a large fantasy world. Across 10 chapters you will explore msytical landscapes, make friend or enemies with many strange and dangerous people, and take on some of the biggest criminals and monsters on the planet. Read More...

Story

In this world each charachter has a story, some only just starting, others which have lasted thousands of years. Your story is not preset, and the way you decide to interact with the charachters and world around you are garenteed to change the way your story plays out.
